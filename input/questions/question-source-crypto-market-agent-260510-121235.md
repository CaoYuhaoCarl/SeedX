# QTM Question Source

## Question
如何用 Agent 搭建一个「分析加密货币市场」的 agent 系统？

## Domain Safety Constraints
- This is an educational/system-design learning question, not investment advice.
- Frame the system as market information monitoring, research summarization, risk awareness, and decision-support notes; do not promise returns.
- Do not design automatic trading or leverage/liquidation recommendation flows as the default.
- Require source attribution, uncertainty labels, market-risk reminders, human review, and guardrails against hype/FOMO.
- Include crypto-specific risks: extreme volatility, liquidity, smart-contract/protocol risk, exchange/custody risk, regulatory/news shock, social-media manipulation, on-chain data interpretation limits, and stablecoin/depeg risk.
